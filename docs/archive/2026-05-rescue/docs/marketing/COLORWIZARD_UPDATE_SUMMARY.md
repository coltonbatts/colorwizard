## Brand Voice Established

### Core Personality
- **Confident without arrogance** — knows what it's good at
- **Direct without rudeness** — straight talk, no corporate polish
- **Irreverent but professional** — can say "bullshit" and mean it
- **Builder talking to builders** — assumes audience understands the work
- **Honest about limitations** — doesn't oversell, doesn't hide what it doesn't do

- ❌ "world-class performance"
- ✅ "50% faster than the alternatives"
